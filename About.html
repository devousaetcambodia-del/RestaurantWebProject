OUR HISTORY

Moha Sambath Restaurant was founded more than 20 years ago as a family-owned business with a passion for sharing authentic Cambodian cuisine. What started as a small local restaurant has grown into a popular dining destination known for its delicious food, friendly service, and welcoming atmosphere. Throughout the years, we have remained dedicated to using fresh ingredients and preserving traditional recipes while creating memorable experiences for every customer who visits us.

MEET OUR CHEF

Our head chef has more than 20 years of culinary experience and a deep passion for Cambodian cuisine. Throughout his career, he has mastered traditional cooking techniques while continuously exploring new ways to enhance flavors and presentation. His dedication to quality and attention to detail ensure that every dish served at Moha Sambath Restaurant meets the highest standards. By combining fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and years of expertise, our chef creates memorable dining experiences that keep customers coming back.

INGREDIENT

At Moha Sambath Restaurant, we believe that great food begins with great ingredients. We proudly source fresh vegetables, herbs, meats, and other ingredients from local farmers and trusted Cambodian suppliers. By using locally grown Khmer products, we support our communities while ensuring that every dish is prepared with freshness, quality, and authentic Cambodian flavors.
